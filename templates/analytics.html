Explainable AI (XAI) & SHAP Explanations

Enterprise AI forecasting shouldn't be a black box. Our platform uses SHAP-like (SHapley Additive exPlanations) attribution to breakdown how individual features influence each prediction:

Previous Month Sales Acts as the baseline momentum factor, determining the structural demand flow.
Pricing & Discounts Elasticity models scale demand. Lower competitor pricing exerts downward pressure.
External Indicators Weather and season coefficients scale grocery or seasonal clothing targets (e.g. Summer uptick).
